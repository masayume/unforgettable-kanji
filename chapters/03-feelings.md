| ТГ╣ (kabanehen) - This radical means "death" or "bare bone." It is found in many kanji related to death, decay, or dying (like ТГ╗). |  \vspace{1.1em} \Large ТГ╣ | \vspace{0.8em} \Huge Т«І | \vspace{1.1em} \Large Тѕћ | This component originally represents two "spears" (Тѕѕ) clashing, implying violence, cutting, or making something small. |
