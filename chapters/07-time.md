Visualize the passage of time through agriculture: \hl{imagine a child (子) standing in a field, watching the stalks of grain (禾) grow from tiny shoots to a golden harvest.}

**Kun\'yomi: キ**

- 季節 (きせつ - kisetsu) - season.
- 四季 (しき / shiki) – the four seasons.
- 夏季 (カキ - kaki) - summer season, summer, summertime.
- 秋季 (シュウキ - juuki) - fall season, autumn season.
